# InterviewAI User Manual

## Getting Started
1. Sign up or log in.
2. Upload your resume to extract skills.
3. Start a new interview session.

## During Interviews
- Answer prompts in the session workspace.
- Monitor time remaining and confidence feedback.
- Submit to receive AI evaluation.

## Analytics
Track your performance over time, identify strengths, and download PDF reports.

## Support
Reach out to InterviewAI support for assistance with subscriptions or platform access.
